✦ If it's a growth opportunity

It happens once or occasionally, not as a pattern
There is an expectation that you might struggle and that is okay
When you ask for support, you get it
When you come out the other side, someone notices

Slide 5

✦ If it's something more deliberate

It happens consistently, as a pattern
You are expected to figure it out and also expected not to struggle
When you do struggle, it becomes a reflection of your capability
The gap in support you were given is never acknowledged

The pattern is what reveals the intention.

One difficult moment is a test. The same difficult moment on repeat, with no support and quiet blame attached, is a choice someone is making.
